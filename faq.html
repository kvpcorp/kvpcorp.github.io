What is an Air Ambulance?

An air ambulance is a critical care helicopter or helicopter that is specially equipped with an approved flight stretcher, specialized medical equipment and carrying drugs and medication to treat a patient. An air ambulance is staffed with a fully trained medical team to handle the specific needs of a patient who needs to be transported by air.
