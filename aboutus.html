About Us
Aviators is a 21 year old company, engaged in Aircraft and helicopter Charter Sales and Management both for domestic and international operations. We provide complete consultancy to startup companies for setting up their operations department, and supply chain for operations.

Lifesaving services are now available as part of Aviators Air Rescue membership.

Partnerships

Aviators Air Rescue is partnering with American Medical Group Holding (AMGH) from Texas, USA. With over 30 years of experience in Medical transport, Aviators will take benefit with the huge amount of experience and database of the latest and most modern safety management systems and online tools. We endeavor to exceed the regulators requirements for compliance and maintain the highest standards of safety across all aspects of our operations.
