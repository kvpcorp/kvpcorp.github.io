STAGE 1
Receipt of Emergency Call

An Aviators Air Rescue Suscriber (or a relative, friend or well-wisher makes a call to our Emergency Response Centre and provides information on the emergency.

STAGE 2
First Aid Advice by HOCC

The call is received by the trained staff at our 24/4, 365 days, Emergency Responce Centre. Qualified emergency physicians assess the condition of the patient.

STAGE 3
Assessment of Transport Requirements

Ground ambulance activated and information is provided to subscriber/patient on the status of the request.

STAGE 4
Patient Stabilization

On reaching the site, subscriber/patient is stabilized by trained paramedics.

STAGE 5
Admission into Nearest Stabilization Centre

Depending on criticality and patient location, the air ambulance is activated.

STAGE 6
Ground Ambulance to Landing Zone

Our highly trained air ambulance crew arrive and load the subscriber/patient into the helicopter and provide pre-arival intimation on condition of subscriber/patient to receiving hospital.

STAGE 7
Air Ambulance Transfer

Aviators Air Rescue helicopter lands at or near the receiving hospital and patient is trasferred to receiving hospital.

STAGE 8
Ground Ambulance Transfer to Hospital
