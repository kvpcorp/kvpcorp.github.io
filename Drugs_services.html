Drugs Transport

Aviators Air Rescue also offers the ability to transfer specialty or lifesaving medicines and drugs in a fast and efficient manner to wherever they are required. This vital service is included to our subscribers and can be availed from us for a variety of requirements and lifesaving medicines which are urgently required can be transferred using Aviators Air Rescue.

You can be rest assured that the proper precautions for handling the medicines will be made by our highly trained crew. This service could also be availed for humanitarian relief requirements for disaster relief or emergency evacuation of the sick and elderly from areas affected by natural calamities.
